Architecture

How the
router thinks.

Five layers of intelligence, running in sequence every time you submit a job queue.

01
Grid carbon fetch
Pulls real-time carbon intensity by region — MISO, CAISO, PJM, ERCOT, NYISO, WECC. Renewable mix, fossil load, and marginal emissions per kWh.
02
Spot price fetch
Live electricity pricing from grid operators. Identifies off-peak windows where compute costs a fraction of peak rates.
03
Claude reasoning
Claude classifies each job — time-sensitive vs. deferrable — and reasons about optimal windows given the combined carbon + cost forecast.
04
Schedule generation
Produces a per-job schedule with start times, carbon savings percentage, and cost savings vs. running immediately.
05
Savings report
Quantifies total CO₂ avoided in kg, dollar savings, and equivalent real-world impact — miles not driven, trees not burned.
