02 — Why Vedura

Built different.
By design.

Local-first intelligence

Every other AI platform sends your data to a corporate cloud. Zen Vision runs entirely on your own hardware. Your conversations, your plant data, your energy patterns — none of it leaves your home.

The whole homestead, not just parts

Competitors solve plant health or solar power. Never both. Zen Vision connects your food and your energy into one intelligent system — because they're not separate in real life.

Solarpunk is a cultural identity — technology that serves people instead of extracting from them. Our users don't just use Zen Vision. They belong to something that a corporation cannot authentically replicate.

Midwest roots

We're building for the people our competitors ignore — rural Ohio, Indiana, Kentucky. We're not pitching to Silicon Valley. We're in Loveland, Ohio, building for our neighbors.
